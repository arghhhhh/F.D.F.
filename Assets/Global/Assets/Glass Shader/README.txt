Use the SKY material to change the background. The Glass material will automatically be updated.
All the textures are properties of textures.com

If the Glass material appears black, do the following:

- Go to Hierarchy.
- Pess right click, select LIGHT menu and create a Reflection Probe.
- In the Reflection Probe component, press the BAKE button.

To manually update the Glass material, press the BAKE button each time you change the texture on the SKY material.
